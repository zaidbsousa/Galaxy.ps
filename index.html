About Galaxy Training Center

Galaxy Training Center is a leading educational institution in Palestine, dedicated to providing high-quality training programs since its establishment in 1997. We offer a wide range of vocational, academic, and specialized courses designed to meet the needs of individuals, businesses, and organizations.

Our mission is to empower students with practical skills and knowledge that prepare them for successful careers in their chosen fields. With over 27 years of excellence and more than 35,000 graduates, Galaxy has established itself as a pioneer in the training sector.
